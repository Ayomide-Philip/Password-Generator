What makes a password strong?

Length

The rule of thumb is that the longer a password, the more secure it is. So, every strong password should be at least 12 characters long.

Complexity

A strong password comprises of a mix of letters, numbers, upper and lower cases, and symbols to create an unpredictable sequence of characters.

Uniqueness

A strong password needs to be unique to the extent that it does not contain any information that can be easily guessed, like words or dates.
